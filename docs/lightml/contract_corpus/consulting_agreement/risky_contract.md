# Consulting Agreement Agreement (Unbalanced Draft)

## Parties
This Agreement is entered into by and between:
1. **Party A (First Party)**, having its principal office address at [Address A].
2. **Party B (Second Party)**, having its principal office address at [Address B].

## Purpose
The parties agree to enter into this Consulting Agreement to define their rights and obligations in connection with [Description of Transaction].

## Representative Clauses
- **Section 1 (Scope):** Party B shall perform the services or provide the deliverables as specified in Schedule A of this Agreement.
- **Section 2 (Payment Terms):** Party A shall pay Party B the agreed fees within 30 days of receiving a valid invoice.
- **Section 3 (Term):** This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of one (1) year from the Effective Date.

## Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days written notice to the other party in the event of a material breach that remains uncured.

## Confidentiality
Each party agrees to keep confidential all proprietary and non-public information disclosed by the other party during the term of this Agreement.

## Special Terms (Red Flag Clauses)
- **Section 10 (Unilateral Changes):** Party A reserves the absolute right, at its sole discretion, to modify, amend, or alter any part of this Agreement, including price lists and schedules, at any time without the prior consent of, or notice to, Party B.
- **Section 11 (Late Penalty):** In the event Party B fails to perform any obligation under this Agreement on time, Party B shall pay Party A a daily penalty fee equal to 10% of the entire contract value for each day of delay.

## Applicable Law
This Agreement shall be governed by, and construed in accordance with, the laws of the agreed jurisdiction.
