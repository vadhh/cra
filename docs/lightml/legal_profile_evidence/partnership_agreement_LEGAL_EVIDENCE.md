- **Competing Profiles**: joint_venture_agreement, commercial_agreement
- **Why They Compete**: Both involve collaboration and profit-sharing terms between business entities.
- **How They Differ**: Partnership agreement establishes an ongoing co-ownership structure to share profits and losses, whereas joint ventures are typically project-specific.
- **Classifier Distinction Strategy**: Check for capital contribution, profit sharing, and management/voting rights.
