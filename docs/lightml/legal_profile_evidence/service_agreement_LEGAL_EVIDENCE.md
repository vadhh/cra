## 8. Competing Contract Types
- **Repository Source**: ldv-backend/detector/profiles/registry_v1.json
- **Repository Object**: competing_profiles NLI overrides
- **Evidence Status**: Repository Verified

- **Competing Profiles**: consulting_agreement, employment_contract, it_service_agreement, general_contract
- **Why They Compete**: Overlap in the performance of services or task execution for compensation.
- **How They Differ**: A service agreement is a standard commercial B2B contract for operational or physical tasks without employer subordination. A consulting agreement is restricted to expert professional advisory services. An IT service agreement is specialized for technical environments (including SLAs and infrastructure management). An employment contract creates a master-servant relationship subject to labor law.
- **Classifier Distinction Strategy**: Analyze the nature of the deliverables: general service contracts focus on operational tasks, consulting agreements focus on strategic/expert advisory, IT services focus on technical metrics, and employment involves operational subordination.
