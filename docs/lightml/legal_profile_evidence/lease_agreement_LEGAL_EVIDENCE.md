## 8. Competing Contract Types
- **Repository Source**: ldv-backend/detector/profiles/registry_v1.json
- **Repository Object**: competing_profiles NLI overrides
- **Evidence Status**: Repository Verified

- **Competing Profiles**: construction_contract, general_contract
- **Why They Compete**: Overlap in real estate covenants, property modifications, or baseline contract covenants.
- **How They Differ**: A lease agreement transfers the right of temporary possession and occupancy of real property in exchange for periodic rent payments. A construction contract governs building, renovation, or engineering work performed on property, with payment structures tied to structural deliverables. A general contract lacks real estate covenants and statutory tenant protections.
- **Classifier Distinction Strategy**: Check if the core object is temporary transfer of possession (lease) versus execution of structural/physical work (construction).
