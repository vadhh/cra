## 8. Competing Contract Types
- **Competing Profiles**: construction_contract, general_contract
- **Why They Compete**: Leases involve property usage which can overlap with construction work performed on a property or general covenants.
- **How They Differ**: Lease agreement grants temporary possession and use of property in exchange for rent, while construction contracts govern building or renovation work.
- **Classifier Distinction Strategy**: Check for landlord/tenant, rent/security deposit, and lease term indicators.

## 9. Disambiguation Criteria
- **Disambiguation Criteria**: Lease agreement grants temporary possession and use of property in exchange for rent, while construction contracts govern building or renovation work. (commercial and legal separation from competing profiles: construction_contract, general_contract).
