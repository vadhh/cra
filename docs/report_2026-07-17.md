### 4b. Corpus Testing & Scale
Engineering acknowledges that the initial 168 controlled fixtures (3 per profile) are only a basic smoke corpus. The roadmap for production-grade validation will eventually require:
* **Adversarial Pairs:** Documents specifically crafted to trigger classifier collisions.
* **Format Diversity:** Scanned, low-quality OCR, and poorly formatted contract text.
* **Layout Variation:** Highly variable headers, footers, and clause structures.

### 4c. Translation Security Details
* **Network Isolation:** In staging/production, LightML is deployed inside the same private VPC subnet; no traffic leaves the internal network.
* **TLS Verification:** Enforced by default; `LDV_LIGHTML_ALLOW_INSECURE` is restricted to local development environments only.
* **Retention Policy:** The translation microservice does not persist contract payloads; translation is performed in-memory, and request bodies are excluded from standard access logs to prevent data leaks.
